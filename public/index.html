1Doge Exciting Features

Holder Rewards

1% of each transaction is redistributed to token holders. That means you can earn more 1Doge Coins by just holding them in your wallet. No need to farm or stake.

1% Liquidity Pool

1% of every transaction is transformed into liquidity for Pancakeswap. It's automatic and helps create a price floor (stability).

Anti-Dump Lock & 4% Sell Fee

Sells are restricted to less than 0.05S% of the total supply + extra 4% fee is applied to all sells. Which means each time You sell, your transaction tax is doubled. This will allow us to reduce swing-trading and break whales' control.

Auto & Weekly Burns

The extra 4% taxed on all sell transactions, will be used to buy back and burn tokens weekly for an ever decreasing circulating supply and increasing price floor.

Secured By Design

Initial liquidity provided would be locked in unicrypt for 6 months and the contract will undergo audits. The results of audit would be published to the community.

Charity Wallet

We have a designated charity wallet that receives redistributions that are used to support our charity partners..

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes 1doge Unique?

1Doge is building a comprehensive platform with Static reflection and play to earn gaming, our game concept is very different from the existing.

Are there any limits on transaction?

There’s no limit on buy transactions, however sell transactions are capped at 0.05% of total supply for every 4 hours.

Any benefit of holding 1doge tokens asides play to earn?

1Doge holders enjoy quite a number of benefits, some of which are : redistribution of 1Doge from static Reflection, increasing price from its hyper deflationary model, platform games participation and much more.

How does the static reflection work?

1% of each sell transaction on the 1Doge network are redistributed instantly and gaslessly to the holders of 1Doge, proportional to their holdings.
